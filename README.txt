== Description ==
Ad Music Player allows you to monetize music on your website. 

You can earn money when someone listen music on your website. Player displays advertisements when the music plays. You can display banners and text advertisements. 
You can display your own Ad or Ad from 3rd party services, such as AdSense.

You can broadcast radio streams and earn money!

You can sell music by adding “Buy now” link to your tracks!

Visit [demo website](http://wpdemo.circlewaves.com/ad-music-player/ "demo website") and [watch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lqIWPmK-RsQ "watch video")

== Installation ==

1. Use native WordPress Plugin Installer or Download and unzip plugin to "/wp-content/plugins/" directory
2. Activate plugin in "Plugins" Menu
3. Configure plugin. [Watch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lqIWPmK-RsQ "Watch video") and see [guidelines and documentation](http://docs.circlewaves.comad-music-player/ "guidelines and documentation")
